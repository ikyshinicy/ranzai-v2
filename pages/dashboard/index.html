RanzAI Quick Tutorial

⌂

Understand your dashboard

Use the dashboard to monitor your account status, Ranz Coin balance, available tools, and latest activity.

  • Check your Ranz Coin balance in the account card or sidebar.
  • Use the history panel to review recent top-ups and tool usage.
  • Open tools directly without leaving the workspace.
◈

Choose and run tools

Select any RanzAI tool from the sidebar or the tools panel. Each tool opens inside this dashboard.

  • Breakdown turns an image into a detailed prompt.
  • Design, Write, Gel, Cut, Convert, and Mockup each have different usage costs.
  • Upload or enter the required input, generate, then download the result.
▰

Top up Ranz Coin

Open Payment, choose a package, and complete the payment. Your coin balance updates automatically after a successful transaction.

  • Click Top Up from the sidebar or account card.
  • Complete payment through the available payment link.
  • Return to the dashboard and wait for the realtime balance update.
